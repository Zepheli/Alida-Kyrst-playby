Alida Kyrst

A short woman with a lot of pride, she looks to be in her late twenties. She carries herself with a cheerful demeanor, but she is quick to anger and yet quicker to hurl insults and fireballs. On her hip is a mythril shortsword, its sheath etched with runes empowering hemocraft. On her hands are numerous small burns and, on her left palm, a particularly large and unhealing gash.
